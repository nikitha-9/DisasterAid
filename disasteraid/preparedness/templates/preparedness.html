Emergency Kits

Learn what to include in your essential emergency kit.

Basic Emergency Kit

  • Water (1 gallon per person per day)
  • Non-perishable food
  • First aid kit
  • Battery-powered radio
  • Flashlight
  • Whistle
  • Dust mask
  • Wrench or pliers
  • Manual can opener
  • Local maps
  • Cell phone with charger
